# President Furniture — What Your Website Does

A plain-language summary of your new website and control panel. No jargon.

---

## In one line

A website that shows off your furniture and custom woodwork, turns visitors
into enquiries, and gives you a private control panel to run all of it
yourself — in English and Bangla.

---

## What your customers can do

- **Browse your furniture** — see photos, prices, and whether an item is in
  stock, all on a fast, modern site that works beautifully on phones.
- **Filter to what they want** — by room, material (solid wood, leather, and
  so on), and availability.
- **Save favourites** — heart the pieces they like into a wishlist.
- **Send you their details** — with one tap, their wishlist and contact
  details come straight to you as a new enquiry.
- **Message you on WhatsApp** — a floating button lets them start a chat
  instantly.
- **Read the site in Bangla or English** — a simple toggle switches the whole
  site.

Your site also makes clear you do **two things**: sell ready-made furniture,
*and* build custom pieces and woodwork on-site (fitted wardrobes, interiors,
doors, and more).

---

## What you can do (your control panel)

You log in to a private admin area to manage everything — no developer needed:

- **Products** — add, edit, or remove items; set prices, stock, and multiple
  photos per piece.
- **Categories** — organise your catalogue.
- **Enquiries (leads)** — see everyone who reached out, mark where each one is
  (new, contacted, won, and so on), jot private notes, and download the whole
  list as a spreadsheet.
- **Change the homepage look** — pick from five ready-made designs with one
  click.
- **Set your contact numbers** — WhatsApp and phone.
- **Manage who can log in** — add staff, change passwords.

---

## How new work with us happens

There's a **"Report an issue / request a change"** button on your site. When
you want something changed or added, you fill it in — it comes to us as a
tracked task, and anything that's new work or a modification is quoted and
billed separately. Nothing gets lost, and you always have a record.

---

## The important bits, simply

- **Always online, nothing to maintain** — it's hosted on professional
  infrastructure that looks after itself.
- **You're in control** — the day-to-day (products, prices, stock, enquiries)
  is all yours to change any time.
- **Instant updates** — changes you make show on the live site immediately.
- **Your data is yours** — enquiries export to a spreadsheet whenever you want.
